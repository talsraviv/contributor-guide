Contributing Matters

We know that Wikipedia would always beat out Encarta. We believe that DuckDuckGo is an incredible distribution platform for an open-source community to change how the web works. We're up against multi-billion-dollar giants - and the only way to avoid becoming them is to be inseparable from the community.

Our first goal is to have 85% of queries covered by Instant Answers (currently this is at 19%).

Our second goal is to have our top 30 languages 100% translated (right now DDG is fully translated in 18 languages).

Improve Our Results

Research

Good Instant Answers are all about accuracy, coverage, and design. Our instant answers benefit immensely from contributors hunting down data sources, sharing their domain knowledge, and solving problems cleverly.

You'll be joining around 200 contributors who actively contribute to idea discussions, finding ways to make new Instant Answers possible.

Start tracking down your first data source

Create Your Own Instant Answers

Want to create the search results you've always wanted? Instant Answers are incredibly fun to code - and rewarding to launch.

Our frameworks and tools make it easy to launch something super fast. Just pick an idea or bring your own.

Make your first pull request by this afternoon

Refine Existing Instant Answers

Have a favorite Instant Answer that you want to make even better? Dive in and modify it. Your pull request could go live in no time.

Want to jump in quickly? We have a list of super low that are a great way to start while pushing live code. Many contributors report completing their first fix in less than two hours after forking the repository.

Pick a Spice “low-hanging fruit” to fix - (or a Goodie)

Improve DuckDuckGo In Your Region

Translate

Help make search privacy accessible to everyone, no matter the language. Start translating DuckDuckGo into the languages you know, and vote on current translations in under a minute. New and updated translations go live every single Friday!

You'll be joining over 2,000 contributors who actively help translate DuckDuckGo into their languages.

Translate just one token (phrase)

Organize Co-Hacking Meetups

Are you part of a community that loves surfing? Protecting the environment? Sports? Music and arts? Health? Finance? Cooking? Education?

Passionate about an area that could use better Instant Answer coverage?

Organizing a meetup is a really fun way to bring people together to build live, open-source Instant Answers together.

We have great, practical advice to help - plus our staff and community leaders will guide you at every step (including live support during the event!).

Launch a Co-Hacking Meetup

Spread the DuckDuckGo Love

You know about DuckDuckGo - but your friends don't yet. Start by telling one person (offline!) why you're excited to have found DuckDuckGo, what your favorite parts are, and why it matters.

You'll be joining over 10,000 fans who have already evangelized DuckDuckGo to their friends and family.
